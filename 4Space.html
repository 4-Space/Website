Planets:

Mercury:

Mercury is the closest planet to the sun and the second planet added to 4-Space, this world has a surface full of craters but is relatively flat. On the surface you will experience 38% gravity and a lot of red slimes, these slimes can be used in red slime blocks which can be used as a 1 block thick airlock. Under the surface you can find Tin, Copper, Gold and Iridium; Iridium can be used in tools and armor which is one of the tools that can remove dungeon bricks.

Venus:

Venus is the second planet from the sun and is the original planet added to 4-Space, the surface does not have many creators but does have large mountains and deep canyons. Venus has a very thick atmosphere and at first glance the sun looks like a snowflake, the surface also has a 90% gravity. Building a unlit base on Venus will result in many Evolved Blazes spawning, but the ore frequency makes up for the danger. While mining you might find Iron, Coal, Tin, Copper, Fossilized Plant, Crystals, Uranium, Sulfur or Red Gem. Sulfur and Red Gem are both used in armor and tool crafting, but only Red Gem can break dungeon bricks. Uranium is compatible with most mods that use Uranium but it is also used in a Uranium battery which holds up to 150MJ of charge.

Gas Giant Moons:

Because it isn’t realistic to land on gas giants, instead you get to land on there much more interesting moons! You can still see them on the map, you just can’t land on them. All of the moons are a huge WIP but I plan on adding Europa, Io, Ganymede, Mimas, Enceladus, Titan, and Triton. In the end they will all have ores and different world generation from each other. Each moon has a different terrain but moons orbiting the same Gas Giant will have similar ores.

FAQ'S:

Q: Where do I submit my bugs? / (why have you now replied through the Contact page?):

A: You should make a account on our site here and submit the bug under the Bug Reports SubForum. Do not use the contact form for bug reports, as I will not answer you. Please make sure to review the bug reporting guidelines on the subforum before posting your bug report.

Q: Can I add this mod to a mod pack?:

A: Yes, as long as credit is given to the original content creator, MattParks5855, as well as a link to this website: mattparks5855.com

Q: How do I give my friend a link?:

A: Simply give them the link to this website, I hope they can find out where to download it, otherwise, I have no hope for them.

Q: Why am I missing planets and moons?:

A: Not all the planets and moons have been rolled out yet. I am designing them to be fully functional before I release them, so it takes a long time.
